# Product Management Team — TOOLS

Research:
- **Tavily Search**: market landscape, competitor analysis, user pain points
- **Firecrawl**: crawl competitor sites, product pages, pricing, feature matrices
- **Wikipedia**: domain context, industry standards

Documentation:
- **Google Docs**: PRD (primary deliverable — goals, stories, metrics, roadmap, out-of-scope)
- **Mermaid**: Product roadmap Gantt chart (milestone timeline)

Tracking:
- **Plane**: Epic per milestone, Story per user story, linked to PRD Doc
- **Jira**: Epics and Stories mirrored to Jira for cross-team visibility

Notifications:
- **Slack**: #product channel — PRD published alert with Doc link
- **Notification**: Stage-complete broadcast before Biz Analysis starts
