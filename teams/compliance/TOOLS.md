# Compliance Team — TOOLS

Documentation:
- **Google Docs**: Compliance & Audit Report (applicable standards, control mapping, evidence catalog)
- **Google Sheets**: Compliance Checklist (requirement, standard, status, evidence, due date, owner)
- **Confluence**: Compliance page published for audit readiness

Tracking:
- **Plane**: Compliance gap issue per failing control, linked to Security Eng findings

Notifications:
- **Slack**: #compliance channel — compliance status summary with gap count
- **Notification**: Stage-complete broadcast to DevOps with infra control requirements
